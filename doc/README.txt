=========
 SVGrafZ
=========

Zope Product to draw charts

Copyright (C) 2003-2010  Michael Howitz, gocept gmbh & co. kg

LICENSE:
  see doc/LICENSE.txt

PURPOSE:
  Draw charts and diagrams from within Zope. SVGrafZ can draw Bar-, Column- and
  Line-Diagrams. The Output it generates is SVG but it may convert the generated
  SVG images to PNG or PDF.
